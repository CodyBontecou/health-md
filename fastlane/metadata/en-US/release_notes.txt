Health.md now has more flexible ways to unlock unlimited exports, alongside the Mac CLI and export reliability improvements in this release.

New
• Choose monthly, yearly, or lifetime Health.md Pro plans from the unlock screen.
• Family monthly, yearly, and lifetime options are available for Apple Family Sharing.
• Install the healthmd command from the Mac app to check readiness and trigger Apple Health exports from scripts or automations.
• Mac can now request exports from an open connected iPhone for yesterday, recent days, or a custom date range.

Fixed
• Existing premium users remain grandfathered, and Restore Purchase keeps checking lifetime, family, subscription, and legacy access.
• Weekly, monthly, and yearly roll-up summaries now write correctly inside archived and zipped exports.
• Mac roll-up settings sync more reliably with the iPhone export pipeline.
• Markdown escaping is safer for metric names, metadata, and roll-up summaries, keeping generated notes readable in Obsidian and other Markdown tools.

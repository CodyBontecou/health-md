Zip exports are here, alongside a redesigned iPad experience.

New
• Zip Export Files bundles your selected formats into one portable archive, including Markdown, Obsidian Bases, JSON, CSV, and the data dictionary.
• Large iCloud or Obsidian vault exports are easier to move because Health.md can write one archive instead of thousands of loose files.
• The iPad app has a refreshed layout for export settings, history, schedule, sync, and account tools.

Improved
• Export format controls are cleaner and easier to scan across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
• The Zip setting now applies to the whole selected export, not only Markdown files.

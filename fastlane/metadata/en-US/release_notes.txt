Health.md can now preserve the original detail behind your Apple Health data, not just daily totals. This gives you a more complete, portable archive in JSON and CSV.

New
• Lossless Health Records captures individual samples and their source, device, timestamps, metadata, and relationships across workouts, sleep, nutrition, medications, blood pressure, clinical records, attachments, and more.
• Export schema v7 adds explicit capture results and warnings, so missing permissions, unavailable records, and partial exports no longer look complete.
• Daily Notes Only updates or creates Obsidian daily notes without adding aggregate files, ZIPs, roll-ups, individual entries, provider sidecars, or a data dictionary.
• Custom schedules can run every few days, weeks, or months from a start date.
• The new {YR} placeholder uses two-digit years such as 26 in export filenames, folders, and Daily Note Injection.

Improved
• Full Access uses one-time Individual and Family Lifetime purchases.
• Saved manual IP connections reconnect securely after the first pairing without requiring a new code.
• Export History explains failures, suggests recovery steps, and keeps technical details available for troubleshooting.
• Large, multi-year, and connected iPhone-to-Mac exports are more resilient.
• JSON provides the complete source archive, CSV includes the same canonical records, and Markdown and Obsidian Bases remain readable daily summaries.
• Individual Entry Tracking uses original HealthKit record identities whenever available.

Fixed
• Corrected VO2 Max summaries, Stand Time and Stand Hours, vitamin and mineral units, blood-pressure pairing, and calendar-timezone roll-ups.
• Improved permission recovery, weekly scheduling, missed-run catch-up, and connected export reliability.

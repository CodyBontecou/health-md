Health.md helps you own your Apple Health history.

Turn sleep, HRV, workouts, steps, vitals, medications, nutrition, mood, and 170+ other Apple Health metrics into private files you control. Export clean Markdown notes for Obsidian, structured JSON for automation, CSV for spreadsheets, or Obsidian Bases frontmatter for queryable dashboards.

WHY PEOPLE USE HEALTH.MD
• Own a backup of years of Apple Health and Apple Watch data
• Fill Obsidian daily notes with sleep, HRV, activity, workouts, medications, symptoms, and vitals
• Build a searchable health journal without creating another cloud account
• Export local files for analysis, automation, spreadsheets, and long-term archiving
• Keep your health records on your devices and in folders you choose

HOW IT WORKS
• Connect Apple Health once
• Choose the metrics and categories you want to export
• Preview the generated daily health note
• Save to an Obsidian vault, iCloud Drive, Files folder, or connected Mac
• Run exports manually, on a schedule, or from Apple Shortcuts

OBSIDIAN-NATIVE HEALTH JOURNAL
Health.md is built for Obsidian users who want body data beside the rest of their notes. Use your existing daily note template, insert health sections into existing notes, customize filenames and folders, and export Obsidian Bases YAML so sleep, HRV, workouts, weight, and more become queryable properties.

EXPORT 170+ HEALTH METRICS
Export across sleep, activity, workouts, heart, HRV, respiratory, vitals, body measurements, mobility, cycling, nutrition, vitamins, minerals, hearing, mindfulness, reproductive health, symptoms, medications, and more.

FLEXIBLE FORMATS
• Markdown for readable daily summaries
• Obsidian Bases/YAML for queryable note properties
• JSON for automation and analysis
• CSV for spreadsheets and notebooks

LOCAL-FIRST PRIVACY
No account. No health-data cloud. Health.md reads Apple Health on your device and writes files directly to the destination you choose. Your health records stay under your control.
